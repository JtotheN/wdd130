Who We Are

We are a non-profit, dedicated group of individuals that are committed to assisting those intrigued by or currently experiencing paranormal phenomena. We have years of experience and want to share what we have learned with you. We would like to be able to assist others in understanding and navigating the territory of the paranormal within their homes or businesses. Furthermore, we provide a platform for private group investigations, allowing enthusiasts to get the chance to possibly explore some of these uncommon occurrences.

We will believe you when no one else will. We have been in your shoes. -Wayne, Founder

Our Mission

Our mission is to passionately support individuals fascinated by or currently encountering paranormal phenomena. Through a dedicated commitment, we strive to be a valuable resource, providing a comprehensive repository of information. Our goal is to empower users, helping them comprehend and navigate the intriguing realms of the paranormal within their homes or businesses. Additionally, we offer a platform for private group investigations, fostering a deeper exploration for enthusiasts into the realm of unconventional occurrences.
