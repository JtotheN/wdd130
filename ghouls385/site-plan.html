Overview

Purpose

The primary aim of this website is to provide a repository of information for individuals with an interest in or currently encountering paranormal occurrences. The website will furnish details about recognizing and dealing with paranormal or unconventional incidents within their homes or businesses. Additionally, the site will facilitate private group investigations for those seeking a more in-depth exploration of such phenomena.

Audience

This website caters to individuals intrigued by the paranormal, as well as those individuals or groups encountering unusual or paranormal phenomena in their residence or workplace.

Content

Home page

We are a Salt Lake City-based non-profit organization with extensive experience dedicated to assisting private residential and business owners. Our mission is to provide comprehensive investigations aimed at determining the presence of paranormal activity or offering rational explanations for unexplained phenomena. We are committed to serving our community with professionalism and compassion.

Images for the Home page

Paranormal Information

Visitors will anticipate finding answers to a range of questions on the site, including how to recognize common signs of paranormal activity, distinguishing between natural and paranormal occurrences, and what steps to take if they suspect their home or workplace is haunted. Visitors might want to read articles and case studies about paranormal experiences, watch videos or listen to podcasts on paranormal investigations. They may also be interested in sharing their own stories and experiences, seeking advice from experts in the field, accessing a forum or community to discuss paranormal topics, and finding recommendations for books, equipment, or resources related to the paranormal.

Images for the Page 2

Investigations

What sets this website apart is its dual role as a dependable resource for the paranormal. It not only provides complimentary private home investigations but also hosts public group investigations, offering participants an opportunity to learn how to use investigation equipment and explore reported paranormal incidents in public spaces. This interactive approach enhances the engagement and depth of understanding for those intrigued by the supernatural. Furthermore, the website fosters a sense of community, where individuals can openly exchange their encounters and connect with others who have faced similar phenomena, creating a unique and supportive environment.
